Let me take you down 'cause I'm going to...

Utilizing over 17,000 drinking straws, 1,000 nails, and nearly 2 miles of fishing line, two curtains of straws were the result of this week long design-build charrette. Hung from branches of an oak tree on the campus of Tulane, the goal was to form an in-between space of light and shadow—surroundings blurred by the translucency of the straws. At night, projected videos created an ethereal experience both in and around the curtains.
