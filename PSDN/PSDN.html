Kevin W Franklin

Metamorphosis

Danaus, the scientific name for a monarch butterfly, was an appropriate name for this semester-long design-build installation that transforms both visually and functionally. Located in the lobby of the Tulane School of Architecture, the installation first appears as a static hexagon broken onto multiple planes. As one progresses up through the stairs, the hexagon breaks apart and morphs into a shelving system outside of the administrative office.
