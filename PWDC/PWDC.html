Facility for Understanding Costal Land Loss

The Wetlands Discovery Center is revolutionizing how we think, teach, and learn about Louisiana's disappearing coast. Our program's focus is on developing adaptation skills in our young people. By doing so, we ensure the survival of our culture along with our workforce. – South Louisiana Wetlands Discovery Center

In order to expand the scope of their outreach, the South Louisiana Wetlands Discovery Center was looking to develop a new facility integrated into a wetlands landscape. Laboratories and exhibit spaces are intended to give students the opportunity to better understand the wetlands enviroment endemic to South Louisiana.

Due to budget limitations, the initial proposal was to construct the facility in four phases as money from donations becomes available.
