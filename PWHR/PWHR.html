Complete Renovation of a Shotgun House

Over the years, numerous renovations transformed this dwelling into a bunch of secluded, cramped, and disconnected spaces.

The strategy for this renovation was to consolidate the kitchen and bathroom into two wood-clad volumes, leaving the space in between free of obstructions and connecting the kitchen with the living spaces.
