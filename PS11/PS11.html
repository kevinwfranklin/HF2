Classrooms & Pods

Located in the Central City neighborhood, the goal of this school is to rethink the relationship between teachers, students, and learning in light of the changes to the New Orleans Public School system after Hurricane Katrina. In response to the City's Master Plan, classrooms are organized into 'pods', connected to a mult-story resource space. This space accommodates events consisting of multiple classes and grades. Accessory functions (cafeteria, offices, band room, etc.) are 'plugged-in' to the central volumes of classrooms.
