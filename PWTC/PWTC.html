New Orleans World Trade Center Redevelopment

In 1967, the World Trade Center Building became the first iconic skyscraper to define the New Orleans' skyline. At the foot of Canal and Poydras Streets, with trains passing underneath, and towering over the Mississippi, it occupies one of the most important sites in the city. Having been abandoned for many years, renewed interest from the City and developers look to give the massive exoskeleton new life.

Stemming from an RFP issued by the City of New Orleans, Perez, as part of a team of developers, developed a proposal for the building's redevelopment.
