Satellite Office for BGW Dental Group

BGW Dental – whose original office is located in Gainesville, GA – purchased this property on the corner of the newly-constructed Friendship Road and State Route 211 in Braselton to be a satellite office. The region is growing quickly, and this intersection provides BGW with a high degree of visibility.

The design strategy is to address the corner lot with a tower over the vestibule that anchors the building on the site. The roof of the lobby is also raised as a means of transitioning from the high tower to the more intimate scale of the treatment space.

Planimetrically, circulation is organized as a loop, with treatment rooms on the perimeter and the service spaces in the core. The conference room is sized to be converted to 4 treatment rooms in the future.
