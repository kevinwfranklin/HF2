New Office for Lim Family Dentistry

The site for this small dental office was the last property to be developed at the end of a row of small medical buildings along Cedarcrest Road. The site was pad-ready and already had a parking lot, however those conditions, along with a retaining wall to the rear of the lot, meant that the buildable area was quite narrow.

The result is an elongated building, which is further lengthened by the addition of a garage. The length is broken up by pilasters between the treatment rooms and herringbone brickwork. The semi-traditional character meshes with the adjacent buildings, and the lobby volume is raised, to provide space for the existing signage which moved from the previous location.
