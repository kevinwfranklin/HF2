Digital Visualizations

The following images are a collection of miscellaneous renderings, both professional and academic, in no particular order. The majority of these were generated between 2008 and 2015 during my time in school and as an intern architect. While I explored a variety of techniques, the general formula followed is 3D Modeling Software (Rhino 3D, Revit) > Rendering Engine (primarily Maxwell Render) > Photoshop > Finished Product.
