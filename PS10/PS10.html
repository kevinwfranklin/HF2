Kevin W Franklin

Gallery + Lofts

The Warehouse District, once a thriving industrial sector in New Orleans, is in the process of gentrification. Old warehouse buildings are being converted into urban lofts and art galleries. This project, set right at the heart of the district, interrogates the relationship between art and living.

Location

New Orleans, LA

Year

Spring 2008

Type

Academic (Second Year)

Program

12 artist lofts, art gallery, coffee shop, & gym

Intersection

The two main elements (galleries + lofts) are intersected at the primary orthoganal corner of the site. This intersection forms an entrance at the public scale to an interior courtyard that functions as a communal space for residents, patrons of the coffee shop, and visitors to the gallery.
